Our Story

Discover the passion and dedication behind SoleSync's premium footwear.

Our Beginnings

Founded in 2010 by footwear enthusiast Michael Johnson, SoleSync started as a small boutique shoe store in downtown Seattle with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, comfortable shoes that don't compromise on style.

What began as a passion project quickly grew into a beloved local destination as word spread about our carefully curated selection and exceptional customer service.

Our Growth

In 2015, we expanded to our online store, allowing us to share our passion for quality footwear with customers across the country. Today, we ship to all 50 states and have a growing international presence.

Despite our growth, we've maintained our commitment to the values that made us successful: quality craftsmanship, ethical sourcing, and personalized service.

Our Philosophy

At SoleSync, we believe that great shoes should combine three essential qualities: comfort, durability, and style. We carefully test every pair we sell to ensure they meet our high standards.

We're committed to sustainability and ethical manufacturing practices. Many of our brands use recycled materials and eco-friendly production methods.
