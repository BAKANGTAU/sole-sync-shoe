Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, shipping, returns, and more.

Shipping times vary depending on your location and the shipping method selected at checkout. Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the continental US, while expedited options are available for faster delivery. International shipping may take 7-14 business days. You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships so you can follow its progress.

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. Please note that customers are responsible for any customs fees, import duties, or taxes that may be charged by their country's customs office. These fees are not included in the shipping cost or product price.

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn items in their original condition with all tags attached. To initiate a return, please visit our Returns Center in your account or contact our customer service team. Return shipping is free for US customers. Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive your return.

Absolutely! We're happy to help with size exchanges. If the item you'd like is in stock, we can process an exchange for you. Simply start a return for the original item and place a new order for the correct size. For faster service, we recommend placing a new order right away and returning the original item separately.

We provide detailed sizing information for each product on its page, including measurements and fit recommendations. If you're unsure, we recommend measuring your foot and comparing it to our size chart. Many of our customers find that our shoes fit true to size, but some styles may run slightly large or small - this will be noted in the product description.

Some of our shoes are waterproof or water-resistant, while others are not. This information is always specified in the product details. Our hiking boots and many winter styles feature waterproof membranes, while most casual shoes and sneakers are not designed to be waterproof. If you need help finding waterproof options, our customer service team can help.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. We also offer financing options through Afterpay and Klarna for qualified customers. All transactions are securely processed with industry-standard encryption.

Yes, your security is our top priority. We use SSL encryption to protect all sensitive information during transmission. We don't store your full credit card details on our servers - all payment information is processed through PCI-compliant payment processors. You can also choose to checkout through PayPal for an additional layer of security.

To reset your password, click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter the email address associated with your account. You'll receive an email with a link to create a new password. If you don't see the email, please check your spam folder. For further assistance, contact our customer service team.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a shipping confirmation email with your tracking number. You can click the tracking link in that email or log into your account to view your order status. If you checked out as a guest, you can track your order using the link in your shipping confirmation email.

Still have questions?

We're here to help! Contact our customer service team for personalized assistance.
